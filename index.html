Soup

Ingredients:

  • Vegetables: 1 medium onion, 3 medium carrots,peeled and sliced, 2 stalks celery, diced, 2 medium potatoes, diced, 1 cup green beans, trimmed and chopped, 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes, .
  • Broth: Vegetable, chicken, or beef stock.
  • Protein (optional): 6 cups vegetable broth (or substitute with chicken/beef stock).
  • Seasonings: 1 teaspoon dried thyme, 2 cloves garlic, 1 bay leaf, Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Oil: 2 tablespoons.
  • Optional: A handful of fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro for garnish.

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Chop and dice all vegetables as listed in the ingredients.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions, garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook for about 5 minutes until they soften.
  3. Add Potatoes and Green Beans: Toss in the diced potatoes and chopped green beans. Stir everything together and sauté for another 2–3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the Tomatoes and Broth: Add the can of diced tomatoes (including the juices) and pour in 6 cups of your chosen broth (vegetable, chicken, or beef). Stir well.
  5. Season the Soup: Add dried thyme, a bay leaf, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Bring the soup to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  6. Simmer Until Tender: Let the soup simmer for about 20–25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender. Stir occasionally.
  7. Taste and Adjust: Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.
  8. Optional Garnish: Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley or cilantro for extra flavor

Breakfast

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 sausages
  • 1 tomato (halved)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup baked beans
  • 2 slices of bread (for toasting)
  • Butter (for the toast)
  • Cooking oil or butter (for frying)

Instructions:

  1. Start with the sausages: Heat a little oil in a large pan and fry the sausages on medium heat until browned and cooked through. Set aside and keep warm.
  2. Bacon: In the same pan, fry the bacon until crispy to your liking.
  3. Tomato and mushrooms: Fry the tomato (cut side down) and mushrooms in the same pan until softened.
  4. Beans: Heat the baked beans in a small saucepan over low heat, stirring occasionally.
  5. Eggs: Fry the eggs sunny-side up (or as you prefer) in a little oil or butter.
  6. Toast: While the eggs are cooking, toast the bread and butter it generously.
  7. Assemble your plate: Arrange everything neatly on a plate, and don't forget to season with salt and pepper to taste.

Lunch

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat is ideal for juiciness)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices (raw or grilled, your choice)
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, and/or mayonnaise (for toppings)
  • Butter (for toasting buns)
  • Cooking oil or butter (for frying patties)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the patties: Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and gently shape them into patties. Press a small indentation in the center of each patty to prevent it from puffing up during cooking. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat with a little oil or butter. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or longer if you prefer them well-done. If you're adding cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking and cover with a lid to let it melt.
  3. Toast the buns: While the patties are cooking, spread butter on the cut sides of the buns and toast them in another pan or on the grill until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the burgers: Start with the bottom bun, add a lettuce leaf, then the patty (with or without cheese), followed by tomato slices, onions, and pickles. Top with your favorite condiments, then place the top bun.
